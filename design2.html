Poster design

Introduction

This is a poster that uses a combination of graphics, angles and colors. It was designed for the movie Inception, following the design style of a famous designer Josef Muller-Brockmann.

Technology Used

Adobe Illustrator: to create theInception poster.

Design Style

This poster is relatively simple in terms of color and graphics. The main content is the four rectangles with colors from deep to shallow to represent the four levels of dreams in the movie. The design style follows Josef Muller-Brockmann who achieved to use simple patterns to convey complicated information.

Analyze

The four rectangles represent the four levels of dreams, and the order of the colors is also from deep to shallow, describing the progress of the plots. In the movie, the protagonist falls into a total of four levels of dreams. Each level of dreams is built on the upper level of dreams. The deeper the color, the deeper the dream, the harder it is to wake up. At the same time, the angle of the entire design is biased to 45 degrees, which also shows spatial instability.

Feedback

The poster uses simple shape combinations and the unique 42 degrees angle, successfully shows the main theory of the movie in an abstract way, following the design style of the famous designer Josef Muller-Brockmann.
