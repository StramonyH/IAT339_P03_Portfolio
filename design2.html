Poster design

Introduce

This is a poster that uses a combination of graphics, angles and colors. What it expresses is a movie called Inception.

Technology

Adobe Illustrator: create Inception poster.

Design Style

This poster is relatively simple in terms of color and graphics. The main content is to express the movie to the audience through four different colors. Use simple design to convey information.

Analyze

The four colors represent different dreams, and the order of the colors is also from light to dark, describing the content of the entire movie. In the movie, the protagonist falls into a total of four levels of dreams. Each level of dreams is built on the upper level of dreams. The deeper the color, the deeper the dream, the harder it is to wake up. At the same time, the angle of the entire design is biased to 45 degrees, which also shows spatial instability.

Feedback

This poster looks simple, but it actually took us a lot of time. We use simple posters to convey more information.
